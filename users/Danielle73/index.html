About me:

Hi there! I'm Danielle, a front-end developer based in London.

I discovered my love for coding during lockdown and have been immersed in front-end development ever since. As someone who has always loved tech but never felt brave enough to pursue it, coding was a perfect way for me to cross that barrier. Since coding my first HTML page, I have loved the challenges and problem-solving that come with the territory. Being able to be creative through code, and seeing my ideas take shape has been hugely satisfying. Though my journey hasn't been linear, I have always found my way back to coding, and hope to develop even further.

After my first Tuesday meet-up session with Founders and Coders (FAC), I immediately knew I had made the right choice in who I wanted to study with. FAC provided a warm, welcoming community, and a supportive, friendly atmosphere that I hadn't experienced before. At FAC, I found a wonderful group of people who shared my passions, who I have collaborated with and become friends with. FAC has been really encouraging in my journey and I hope to continue growing and contributing to this amazing community.
